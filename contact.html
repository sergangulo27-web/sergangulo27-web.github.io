Prayer Requests

I ask for your prayers in these specific areas of my mission:

  • For the people I meet on the streets — that they would feel truly seen and know God's love through each encounter.
  • For my spiritual strength and endurance as I carry the weight of others' stories each day.
  • For full financial support — that God would raise up a generous community to sustain this mission year-round.
  • For the city of Denver — that hearts would be opened to the dignity of every person, housed or not.
  • For my family and loved ones who support me from a distance through this calling.
